About Us

The Mwangaza Mentorship Program was founded in 2020 with the mission of empowering students through mentorship in STEM, academics, and ecopreneurship. Our goal is to inspire the next generation of innovators and leaders by providing them with the skills, knowledge, and guidance they need to succeed.

Our Mission

To foster a culture of excellence and innovation by connecting students with mentors who can guide them in their academic and entrepreneurial journeys.

Our Vision

To create a world where every student has access to quality mentorship and the resources they need to achieve their dreams.

Our Programs

STEM Mentorship Hands-on learning in science, technology, engineering, and mathematics.
Academic Mentorship Guidance in academic subjects and career planning.
Ecopreneurship Training in sustainable business practices, including coconut product creation.

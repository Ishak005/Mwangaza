About Mwangaza Mentorship Program

Since 2020, the Mwangaza Mentorship Program has been a beacon of hope and opportunity for students across Kenya. We are committed to empowering the next generation through innovative mentorship in STEM, academics, and ecopreneurship. Our programs are designed to unlock potential, foster creativity, and build sustainable futures.
